Central Facilities

Lecture Theatres
Lecture Theatres

We have two fully Air conditioned lecture theatre of 180 capacity each with Audio Visuals Aids.

Library Facilities
Library Facilities

Central Library is located in a spacious centrally Air Conditioned building adjacent to Medical College. It has more than 3500 books of all relevant subjects. It also has more than 20 National and International Journals along with e-Library facility. Adequate internal and external reading rooms for students and faculties available separately.

Demo Rooms
Demo Rooms

Demonstration Rooms are available for Anatomy, Physiology, Bio-Chemistry and Community Medicine with adequate sitting capacity for each department in Medical College. They are equipped with modern AV aids for teaching. In OPD, separate Demonstration Rooms are available for all clinical departments. Similar Demonstration Room is available for Department of Anaesthesia in Hospital Building.

Hostels
Hostels

Separate hostel for Boys and Girls are situated inside the campus. Both AC and Non-AC rooms with triple & double sharing are available. All rooms are equipped with attached toilets. Each hostel has separate common room with indoor games and recreation room with entertainment facilities. Separate mess for boys and girls are available in their respective hostels. Uninterrupted power and water supply are available round the clock.

Sports & Games
Sports & Games

Both Indoor and Outdoor sports facilities are available. Spacious playground for Football, Cricket, Badminton, Volleyball are there. Indoor games like Table Tennis, Carrom Board and Multi Gym are also available,

Cafeteria
Cafeteria

Canteen facility for students and faculties are available inside the campus. This primarily serves Indian cuisines. Another fast food counter and coffee corner are located closed to the OPD building.
