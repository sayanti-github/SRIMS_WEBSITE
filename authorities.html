Sri Tapan Kr. Pobi
Founder Trustee
Message from Founder Trustee

I am delighted to welcome you to the College Website. Here you will find all relevant information about our Institute- the Infrastructure, departmental Faculties and staffs, Academic programmes, admission procedures and its related aspects. Our college is affiliated to West Bengal Health University and it is permitted by Medical Council of India for running the MBBS course with 150 annual intake. Outstanding features of our college is excellence of our teaching faculty. They are talented, committed successful Physicians, Surgeons and Basic Science Teachers. The attached service hospital with a lot of clinical material and State of the Art Infrastructure adds great value to this institute. In choosing to send your son or daughter to SRIMS & SH, you are making the best decision regarding their professional education. However, if you require further information and would like to visit our college, feel free to contact us.

Prof. (Dr) Bonapart Chowdhury
Chief Executive Officer

Prof. (Dr) Bonapart Chowdhury is an eminent ENT and Head & Neck Surgeon with special interest in Thyroid surgery. He has graduated from the prestigious Institution- Sri Ramachandra Medical College & Research Institute, Chennai and obtained his Master of Surgery in ENT and Head & Neck Surgery from Agartala Government Medical College & GB Pant Hospital, Agartala. He has many national and international publications as well as national and regional presentations to his credit. Prof. (Dr) Chowdhury is an excellent medical teacher, examiner as well as a gifted orator with exceptional leadership qualities. He was instrumental in establishing this Institute as well as contributed very significantly in the growth of this Medical College and Hospital. Prof. Bonapart is currently heading the department of ENT and leading this Institution as the Chief Executive Officer.
